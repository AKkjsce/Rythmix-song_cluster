Sound Pattern Insights

Rythmix analyzes audio features like - energy, tempo and mood - to automatically organize tracks into distinct sound profiles, focusing entirely on how the music feels rather than genre labels.

Sound Space Map

Each point represents a song. The model initially analyzes multiple audio features, but since those can't be visualized directly, PCA reduces them to two dimensions. Songs with similar sound characteristics appear closer together on the plot.

Vibe Signatures

Each radar chart summarizes the average audio features of a cluster. Peaks in attributes such as energy indicate high-intensity tracks, whereas higher acousticness values correspond to more stripped-down and mellow songs.

Cluster Selection using Model Criteria

We didn't rely on guesswork — a Gaussian Mixture Model was used to evaluate different cluster counts (K = 6 to 15), scoring each configuration to identify the most suitable one.

  • BIC Score (red line): Bayesian Information Criterion. It applies a penalty for increasing model complexity, meaning that lower scores represent a more optimal trade-off between fit and the number of clusters.
  • AIC Score (blue dashed line): Akaike Information Criterion. Like BIC, it evaluates model fit while penalizing complexity, though less strictly. A lower score reflects a more suitable model.
  • Orange dotted line: The point representing the optimal cluster count, where the model best balances goodness of fit with model complexity.
